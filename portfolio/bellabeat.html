How Can a Wellness Technology Company Play It Smart?

Portfolio Project • Bellabeat Case Study • R and Jupyter Notebooks

A hypothetical scenario where I am working at Bellabeat, a high-tech manufacturer of health-focused products for women. In this case study, I analyzed user activity and wellness datasets using R and developed a stakeholder presentation with actionable recommendations.

The project focused on turning fitness and usage data into practical product and marketing insights. This included data cleaning, exploratory analysis, identifying behavioral trends, and creating clear visual summaries for decision-makers.
